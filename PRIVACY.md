# Privacy Policy for Prompt for Prompt

Prompt for Prompt (the "Extension") is a productivity tool designed to optimize user text into AI prompts. We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how your data is handled.

## Data Collection and Usage
The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any unauthorized third parties. 

To function correctly, the Extension processes the following data:
- **Text Input**: The text you explicitly choose to optimize is captured and sent to your chosen AI provider (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, or your local server). 
- **Authentication Data**: API keys provided by the user are stored locally in your browser's secure storage and are only used to authenticate requests to the AI providers you specify.
- **Settings**: Your preferred keyboard shortcuts and presets are stored locally on your device.

## Data Transmission
All text processing and transmission occur directly between your browser and the AI API endpoints you have configured. No data is routed through any servers owned by the developer.

## Third-Party Services
The Extension interacts with third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, etc.). Your data is subject to the privacy policies of the specific provider you choose to use.

## No Sale of Data
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties for marketing or any other purposes.

## Contact
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository where this extension is hosted.
